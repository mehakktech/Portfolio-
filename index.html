About Me

Designing with purpose and passion

I'm a self-taught web designer on a journey to master the craft of creating beautiful, user-friendly digital experiences. What started as curiosity has turned into a passion that I'm committed to developing every day.

My approach combines aesthetic sensibility with functionality. I believe good design should not only look appealing but also solve problems and enhance user experience.

Currently, I'm focused on strengthening my foundation in HTML and CSS while exploring UI/UX principles and JavaScript frameworks like React. My goal is to grow into a well-rounded designer who can bring creative visions to life through code.

When I'm not designing, you can find me browsing design inspiration galleries, learning new techniques through online courses, or experimenting with personal projects.
